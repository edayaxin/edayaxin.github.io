Painting / Drawing

Pine and Stream

Chinese Painting, 120 cm x 40 cm, 2016

In the tradition of Chinese Literati, Pine represents the strong will of a person in face of difficulties. Over thousands of years, painting is a way to connect nature via movements of brushes, shapes of strokes and thickness of ink. My practice of Chinese Art is to understand nature from the perspective of ancient Chinese philosophy, which seeks the unity of human being and nature. Pine and Stream is an imitation painting from Chen Shaomei in the early 20th century.





Bath of Ten Suns

Oil on Canvas, 70 cm x 100 cm, 2015

The painting depicts the methodology “Bath of Ten Suns” from Shan Hai Jing. Xihe is the solar deity and mother of ten suns. To prepare her children for the duty, she bathes them in the East Sea and sends them to the heaven with her vehicle. Among the river and mountains, Xihe sits under a tree and the color of the suns are reflected in the clouds and water. The painting is an imagined scene of nature in methodology.





Seed in the sea

Drawing, 80cm x 100cm, 2016

The fruit was picked up near the seashore of Sai Kung in Hong Kong. The drawing was to trace back its life in the sea.
